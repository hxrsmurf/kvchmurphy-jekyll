Android Police reports that Google will be retiring the popular Google Now launcher Q1 2017. Current devices that have the launcher will be able to keep it. The launcher will be removed from the Google Play Store.

The Google Search app has some of the code from the aforementioned Launcher. While the Pixel Launcher, for the Google Pixel and Pixel XL phones, uses the new Google Assistant.
