My friend asked to see a hour-by-hour comparison of himself (Colin) and Brandon 1. We can see that Brandon 1 did not send anything at 0300 whereas Colin did not send anything at 0300 and 0500. We neither sent nor received at 0400...turns out we do sleep.

Brandon 1 sent the most which Chris following up: 690 and 449 respectively. I sent to Brandon 1 and Chris the most, too: 940 and 668 respectively.

I send mostly at 0100 (487) and 1100 (340) and on Mondays (874) and Sundays (766).

On Monday, I send mostly around 0000 and 0100! As Monday's are my weekends and I try to wake up early Monday morning, it is strange that I am still up at these times.

With all of that said, I am glad I am trying to check different "items" each month. It is getting difficult trying to determine what to find. Additionally, some of these numbers are skewed because if we are having a conversation, a particular hour may be higher than before. I am sure there is a way to filter out <1-2 minute responses, but that may be bad because what if they are different types of messages?

I would also like to welcome Steven to the top ten list!

Finally, this might look and feel like I am ranking my friends...but I am mostly just keeping track because I can? I was thinking I should track sports stats, like Formula 1 lap times. Maybe I will do both. I guess I just enjoy seeing what days and hours I speak with my friends.
