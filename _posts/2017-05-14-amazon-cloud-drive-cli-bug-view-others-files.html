I just wanted to make you aware of a small bug that allows one to view other Amazon Cloud Drive (ACD) even though it is not authenticated.

First your DB somehow gets corrupted. Delete it, acdcli sync, then tada.

Please be wary of using ACD_CLI utility until it is patched. And it is definitely recommend to use encryption!
