According to Fox Business, Ford is recalling vehicles that may have a shifter cable detach while in park. This will cause the vehicle to roll-away. There are no warning indicators in the vehicle suggesting the car is not in park. Ford is not aware of any accidents at this time.

  • 2013 to 2016 Ford Fusion
  • 2013 to 2014 Ford Escape
