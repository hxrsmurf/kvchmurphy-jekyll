I recently took a gorgeous photo of the sunset off of Lake Michigan in South Haven. A friend suggested I make a poster out of it. I had to look up the standards for posters. Apparently, the standards are 300 pixels per inch (PPI). One divides each dimension by 300 and that will give you a rough estimate on the poster size.
