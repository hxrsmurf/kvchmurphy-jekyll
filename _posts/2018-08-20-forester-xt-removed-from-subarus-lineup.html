Initially captured by Torque News from Top Gear Philippines, Subaru says it has three reasons why they removed the 2.0 litre turbocharged

  1. Sportiness is not about more power
    • Responsiveness of engine and handling from the driver inputs
  2. New Subaru Global Platform (SGP)
    • Use new, existing SGP Impreza and Crosstrek shared platform
  3. All Foresters have new eyes
    • SGP has Subaru Eyesight, standard
